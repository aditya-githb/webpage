ALL GAMES ALL ASSASSIN'S OFFICIAL SITE PHOTOS

Assassin's Creed

Assassin's Creed (2007) introduces Altair Ibn-La'Ahad, an Assassin navigating the Holy Land during the Third Crusade. Engaging in stealthy parkour and historical intrigue, Altair uncovers a plot involving ancient artifacts. The game's innovative narrative and open-world design marked a milestone in gaming history.

Assassin's Creed II

Assassin's Creed II (2009) follows Ezio Auditore da Firenze in Renaissance Italy seeking vengeance for his family. Enhanced gameplay, a rich storyline, and historical accuracy characterize this sequel, setting a new standard for action-adventure games.

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood (2010) continues Ezio Auditore's journey in Rome as he battles the Templars. Introducing the Brotherhood system, it refines gameplay, expands the narrative, and solidifies the franchise's reputation for compelling storytelling and open-world exploration.

Assassin's Creed Revelations

Assassin's Creed Revelations (2011) concludes Ezio Auditore's saga. Set in Constantinople, it intertwines Ezio's quest with Altair Ibn-La'Ahad's memories. The game enhances the series' narrative complexity, blending historical fiction, action, and stealth.

Assassin's Creed III

Assassin's Creed III (2012) follows Ratonhnhaké:ton, or Connor, during the American Revolution. His journey spans complex allegiances, featuring historical figures and events. The game introduces naval gameplay and explores themes of freedom and justice.

Assassin's Creed Liberation

Assassin's Creed III: Liberation (2012) stars Aveline de Grandpré, an Assassin of mixed heritage in 18th-century New Orleans. Uncover conspiracies, navigate societal challenges, and experience a unique perspective in this portable addition to the Assassin's Creed saga.

Assassin's Creed IV Black Flag

Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag (2013) follows Edward Kenway, a charismatic pirate and Assassin. Set in the Golden Age of Piracy, sail the Caribbean seas, plunder ships, and uncover a Templar plot. The game combines naval exploration with a rich open world.

Assassin's Creed Rouge

Assassin's Creed Rogue (2014) explores the perspective of Shay Patrick Cormac, an Assassin turned Templar. Navigate the icy waters of the North Atlantic and New York City, experiencing the Assassin-Templar conflict from a unique angle.

Assassin's Creed Unity

Assassin's Creed Unity (2014) is set in Paris during the French Revolution. Play as Arno Dorian, navigating political turmoil and uncovering conspiracies. The game emphasizes cooperative multiplayer, offering a visually stunning recreation of historical Paris.

Assassin's Creed Syndicate

Assassin's Creed Syndicate (2015) transports players to Victorian London. Twins Jacob and Evie Frye lead the fight against oppression, introducing new gameplay mechanics like the rope launcher and emphasizing a dynamic open-world experience.

Assassin's Creed Origins

Assassin's Creed Origins (2017) explores the origins of the Assassin Brotherhood in ancient Egypt. Bayek, a Medjay, seeks revenge, unraveling a conspiracy. The game introduces RPG elements, a vast open world, and a revitalized combat system, breathing new life into the series.

Assassin's Creed Odyssey

Assassin's Creed Odyssey (2018) ventures into ancient Greece, offering players the choice between siblings Alexios and Kassandra. Embracing RPG elements, the game unfolds an epic odyssey with branching narratives and naval exploration, expanding the franchise's immersive open-world experience.

Assassin's Creed Valhalla

Assassin's Creed Valhalla (2020) immerses players in Viking culture, following Eivor's journey as they lead their clan to settle in England. The game blends action, exploration, and political intrigue, with stunning visuals and a rich narrative, showcasing the series' evolution.

Assassin's Creed Mirage

Assassin's Creed Mirage (2023) is a narrative-driven action-adventure game. You play as Basim, a street thief turned Master Assassin, exploring the vibrant city of Baghdad during its Golden Age.
